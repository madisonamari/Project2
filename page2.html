Polo G

Polo G, real name Taurus Tremani Bartlett, is a 22-year old American rapper from Chicago,Illinois. He is best Know for his song, "Pop Out" featuring Lil Tjay, which peaked at number eleven on the Billboard Hot 100. His second studio albulm, The GOAT, debuted at numbger one on the Billboard 100 and was certified platnium by the RIAA.

Lil Baby

Lil Baby started his career in 2017, releasing several mix tapes and singles. His 2017 single "My Dawg" was his breakthrough hit, reaching number 35 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. His 2018 mixtape Harder Than Ever included the single "Yes Indeed" featuring Drake, which peaked at number 6 on the Billboard Hot 100.

Cardi B

Cardi B is an American rapper,songwriter,television personality, and actress. She first gained recognition in 2015 when she began posting videos of herself rapping on Instagram. In 2017, she signed her first major label deal with Atlantic Records. She has multiple hits some including: Boday Yellow, I Like it, Bongos, and so many more.
