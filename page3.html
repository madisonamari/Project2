My Absolute Favorite

I can honestly say I believe my most favorite food has to be Red Beans And Rice. I can exactly explain why I am always drawn to this meal. My family likes to try to blame it on my "old soul."I dont know if I like this meal because it reminds me of my Granny or because the flavors in it are just so exquisite especially when it's all taken in one bite.
